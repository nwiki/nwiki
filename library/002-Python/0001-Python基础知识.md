## 数组

### 定义

    >>> array = [9, 8, 7, 6, 5, 4, 3, 2, 1, 0]
    >>> print(array[5])
    4
    >>> for item in array:
    ...     print(item, end=' ')
    ...
    9 8 7 6 5 4 3 2 1 0 
    >>> 
    >>> for (index, item) in enumerate(array):
    ...     print(index, item)
    ...
    0 9
    1 8
    2 7
    3 6
    4 5
    5 4
    6 3
    7 2
    8 1
    9 0
